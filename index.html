About The Artist

Dahn Bi Chong is currently a student at the University of Colorado Boulder studying in the creative technology and design major. She has a passion for many kinds of fine art as seen in the gallery to the right that contains four of her favorite works in the past 3 years. Despite her love for fine arts, recently Chong has developed an interest in game design. She hopes to work in concept or character design and animation in the future at companies such as Blizzard or Riot, the creators of her favorite games League, Valorant and Overwatch.
